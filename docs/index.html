Technology

The intelligence inside

Proprietary AI and sensor systems engineered for real-world performance — not lab conditions.

RTK Precision Navigation

Real-time kinematic GPS positioning delivers 2cm accuracy. Your robot always knows exactly where it is and optimises every route.

AI 3D Obstacle Avoidance

Time-of-Flight and ultrasonic sensors work together to detect and avoid obstacles in real time — pets, toys, furniture, and more.

Full App Control

Schedule, monitor, and manage your robots from anywhere via the mobile app. Set zones, track progress, and receive real-time alerts.

25% Longer Battery Life

LiFePO4 and automotive-grade Li-Ion cells with faster charging than any leading competitor. Run longer, charge faster, last longer.

Auto Return & Resume

Automatically docks when battery is low, then resumes from the exact same spot. No missed coverage, ever.

Solar & Sustainable Power

Our pool skimmer runs on integrated solar panels. Eco-friendly operation with near-zero running costs and a minimal carbon footprint.
