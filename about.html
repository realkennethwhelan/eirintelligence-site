About Eirintelligence

Built in Ireland.
Built to actually work.

We started with a simple frustration: smart-home robots that looked great in demos but failed in real gardens and real pools. So we built something better — practical, AI-powered machines that do the job, day after day.

Our Story

Practical robots for real life

Eirintelligence was founded with one conviction: that AI-powered robots should earn their place in your home by being genuinely useful — not impressive on paper, but unreliable in practice.

Our first product, the Robotic Lawn Mower, was designed from the ground up for real European gardens — uneven terrain, unpredictable weather, and the kind of daily wear that most consumer robots can't handle. We followed it with the Robotic Pool Skimmer, a solar-assisted autonomous cleaner built for pools of all sizes.

Every product we ship is the result of extensive field testing. We don't release until it works — consistently, reliably, season after season.

Mission & Values
Why we exist
"Intelligent robots that actually work for you — not just in the showroom, but in your garden, at your pool, and through every season."

Reliability First

A robot that fails halfway through your lawn is worse than no robot at all. We test obsessively so our products perform every single time — in rain, heat, and everything between.

Real-World Design

Our engineers test in actual homes and gardens, not controlled labs. Every design decision is driven by how the product performs in the conditions our customers actually live in.

Sustainable by Design

From solar-assisted pool skimmers to modular, repairable components — we build for longevity. Less waste, longer life, and a smaller footprint for your outdoor space.

Genuine Intelligence

AI isn't a buzzword here — it's how our robots learn your space, adapt to obstacles, and optimise routes over time. Smart navigation that actually makes a difference.

Customer Commitment

We're a small, focused team that stands behind every product we sell. Real human support, clear warranties, and an honest commitment to putting things right if something goes wrong.

Built to Last

Modular components mean our robots can be serviced and upgraded — not thrown away. We design against planned obsolescence because that's better for you and better for the planet.

Our Roots

Proudly Irish,
globally minded

Ireland has long been a hub for technology, engineering, and innovation — and Eirintelligence is proud to be part of that tradition. Our name carries the Irish word Éire, and our commitment to quality reflects the standards we hold ourselves to.

Being based in Ireland means we operate under EU consumer protection and safety regulations — among the most rigorous in the world. When you buy from Eirintelligence, that's the standard behind every product.

The United States is one of our most important markets. Our robots are priced and shipped to serve US customers directly, and we're committed to providing the same level of after-sales support whether you're in Dublin or Denver.
